The Five Gaps

Your integration map named exactly what's missing today. These five gaps are the core deliverables of this build — everything else already exists in some form and needs to be connected and standardized.

Gap 01

Referrals & reviews loop

Happy clients aren't systematically converted into reviews and referred friends feeding back into traffic.

Gap 02

VA / admin audit layer

No daily routine catching missed follow-ups, unconfirmed bookings, or incomplete onboarding before they cost a client.

Gap 03

Renewal / win-back tracking

Renewals, churn risk, and win-back outcomes aren't tracked anywhere — revenue leaks quietly at the end of packages.

Gap 04

Red-flag escalation

Missed check-ins, late sessions, and unhappy-client signals have no defined path to someone accountable.

Gap 05

Source-of-truth rules

When data disagrees between platforms, there's no agreed answer for which system wins.

The build in one sentence: close these five gaps by wiring GHL, GymMaster, Trainerize, and Monday together with clear handoffs, a daily audit routine, and exception alerts.

Source of Truth

Each function lives in exactly one system of record. Every automation and report in this build respects these rules.

GoHighLevel
Owns

Leads, follow-up, and the conversion pipeline. Every inbound inquiry starts and is nurtured here.

GymMaster
Owns

Billing, scheduling, and attendance. Packages, Stripe payments, sessions, and credits live here.

Trainerize
Owns

Coaching, plans, and check-ins. Nutrition, mobility, training plans, and weekly client check-ins.

Monday
Owns

Oversight, accountability, and exceptions. Dashboards, red flags, renewals, and team tasks.

The one rule: data may be mirrored into another system for visibility — a lead's status can appear in Monday — but it is only ever edited in its home system. This is what keeps four platforms from becoming four conflicting databases.

Client Lifecycle

Your Team Wireframe, operationalized. Each stage below defines what the system does automatically, who owns the human checkpoint, and what alert fires if something slips. Click a stage to explore.

Acquire

System of record: GoHighLevel

All traffic — Facebook/Instagram ads, Google, organic, referrals — routes to the website and lead forms. Every lead is captured into GHL with its source attributed and enters the pipeline automatically. An instant text/email follow-up fires the moment a lead comes in, and the nurture sequence continues until a consult or trial is booked.

VA / AdminAudits lead response speed daily.
ManagementReviews lead flow and coaching capacity.
Exception alertA lead sits past the agreed response window with no follow-up → Monday alert with an owner.

Book Intro

System of record: GoHighLevel → GymMaster

The free intro week is booked through GHL's booking workflow with automated confirmations and reminders. The booking syncs to GymMaster as a scheduled appointment so coach availability and the intro experience stay aligned.

CoachKeeps availability current; preps the intro experience.
VA / AdminConfirms every booking and verifies reminders sent.
Exception alertA booked lead has no confirmed appointment → alert before the slot is lost.

Convert

System of record: GymMaster + Stripe

The client chooses a package and pays. GymMaster and Stripe handle payment and recurring billing; GHL marks the pipeline conversion so lead-source attribution survives the sale.

CoachRuns the consult and assessment; supports the close.
VA / AdminVerifies package, payment, and client ID match.
ManagementApproves any exceptions or discounts.
Exception alertA sale recorded without verified payment or package → alert.

Onboard

System of record: Trainerize + Monday

After the sale, the client is added to Trainerize: app invite, consultation intake form, and a nutrition + mobility/training plan built within 24 hours. Intake, tasks, and ownership are tracked in Monday so nothing depends on memory.

CoachBuilds the nutrition and mobility plan within 24 hrs.
VA / AdminVerifies app setup and intake completion.
ManagementEscalates missed setup deadlines.
Exception alertOnboarding incomplete past 24 hrs → escalation to management.

Deliver + Monitor

System of record: Trainerize + GymMaster → Monday

Clients train and complete weekly check-ins in Trainerize; attendance and sessions remaining live in GymMaster. Check-in completion, missed sessions, and red flags flow into Monday's accountability and client-risk dashboards.

CoachRecords session outcomes; reviews weekly check-ins.
VA / AdminAudits attendance, check-ins, surveys, and red flags.
ManagementMonitors accountability, client risk, and payroll inputs.
Exception alertMissed check-in, late/missed session, or unhappy-client flag → alert with a named owner.

Renew + Grow

System of record: Monday + GHL + GymMaster

Renewal outcomes and attribution are tracked across Monday, GHL, and GymMaster. At renewal or a results milestone, the review/referral ask fires automatically — closing the growth loop back into your traffic sources. Win-back sequences catch clients who lapse.

CoachReassesses goals; presents the next-phase plan; asks for the review/referral.
VA / AdminTracks renewal outcomes; logs win-back / offboard notes.
ManagementReviews retention, churn, capacity, and commissions.
Exception alertRenewal window passes with no recorded outcome → alert.

Build Plan

Four phases, each independently testable. Every phase ends with a live walkthrough against the success criteria and your sign-off before the next begins.

Phase 1

GHL Lead Engine

Lead capture forms, pipeline stages, source tracking, instant + nurture follow-up automations, and the intro-week booking workflow. The foundation everything else hangs off.

Gate: test lead from every source flows capture → follow-up → booked intro.
Phase 2

Handoffs

Sale in GHL creates the client in GymMaster with package + billing; onboarding triggers into Trainerize with the 24-hour plan workflow.

Gate: end-to-end test purchase becomes an onboarded Trainerize client.
Phase 3

Monday Oversight + Audit Layer

Master client and coach accountability dashboards, exception/red-flag automations, renewals board, and the VA/admin daily audit SOP.

Gate: simulated exceptions surface in Monday; VA completes a full audit cycle.
Phase 4

Growth Loops + Reporting

Referral/review automation, renewal + win-back tracking, owner summary report, source-of-truth one-pager, and role SOPs with a team walkthrough.

Gate: full lifecycle runs on real clients with reporting live.

What Success Looks Like

Nine verifiable outcomes. We test each one live together — no "trust me, it works."

✓
Every lead lands in GHL with source attribution and enters the pipeline automatically.
✓
No lead sits without follow-up — instant response fires, and the sequence runs until a consult is booked.
✓
A sale in GHL creates the client in GymMaster with package and billing — no re-typing.
✓
Every new client is set up in Trainerize with intake and plans inside 24 hours.
✓
Every exception — missed follow-up, check-in, session, or red flag — appears in Monday with an owner and deadline.
✓
Renewal, churn, and win-back status is tracked and reviewed on a set cadence.
✓
Review and referral requests fire automatically at the right moments.
✓
The VA/admin runs a documented daily audit with dashboards built for it.
✓
The whole team works from one source-of-truth reference — no more conflicting records.
